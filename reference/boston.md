# Monthly dollar volume of sales

Monthly dollar volume of sales on Boston stock exchange and combined New
York and American stock exchange. January 1967 – November 1969.

## Usage

``` r
boston
```

## Format

Bivariate time series containing the following columns:

- nyase:

  New York and American Stock Exchange dollar volume

- bse:

  Boston Stock Exchange dollar volume

## Source

Makridakis, Wheelwright and Hyndman (1998) *Forecasting: methods and
applications*, John Wiley & Sons: New York. Exercise 6.5

## References

McGee and Carleton (1970) Piecewise regression, *Journal of the American
Statistical Association*, **65**, 1109–1124.

## Examples

``` r
plot(boston)
```
