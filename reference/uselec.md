# Total generation of electricity

Monthly total generation of electricity by the U.S. electric industry
(Jan 1985 - Oct 1996.

## Usage

``` r
uselec
```

## Format

Time series data

## Source

Makridakis, Wheelwright and Hyndman (1998) *Forecasting: methods and
applications*, John Wiley & Sons: New York. Exercise 7.8.

## Examples

``` r
plot(uselec)

seasonplot(uselec)

tsdisplay(uselec)

```
