# Housing data

Monthly housing starts, construction contracts and average new home
mortgage rates (Jan 1983 - Oct 1989).

## Usage

``` r
housing
```

## Format

Trivariate time series containing the following columns:

- construction:

  Construction contracts (millions of dollars)

- interest:

  Average new home mortgage rates

## Source

Makridakis, Wheelwright and Hyndman (1998) *Forecasting: methods and
applications*, John Wiley & Sons: New York. Chapter 8.

## References

Survey of current business, US Department of Commerce, 1990.

## Examples

``` r
plot(housing)

```
